## ğŸ’¡ Pro Tips

### For All Platforms:
1. **Engage immediately** - First hour is critical
2. **Ask questions** - End with "What would you automate?"
3. **Show results** - Metrics matter (60%, $3K, etc.)
4. **Be authentic** - Share your real experience
5. **Help others** - Reply with helpful info, not just thanks

### Platform-Specific:
**LinkedIn**:
- Longer posts work better
- Professional tone
- Use line breaks for readability
- More hashtags OK (10-12)

**Twitter**:
- Threads > single tweets
- Conversational tone
- Fewer hashtags (2-3 max)
- Tag @AnthropicAI in a reply

**Reddit**:
- Value-first, not promotional
- Detailed technical content
- Engage in comments extensively
- No hashtags
